About This Project

This research project explores competitive Rocket League gameplay, analyzing player strategies, team dynamics, and performance metrics to better understand high-level competitive play.

Research Focus

  • Player positioning and rotation patterns
  • Goal-scoring mechanics and prediction
  • Team coordination metrics
  • Rank progression analysis

Data & Findings

Our analysis includes thousands of match replays and player statistics. Check back for detailed findings and visualizations.
